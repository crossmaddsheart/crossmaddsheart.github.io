The data I found included the surface temperatures from all over the world, but I decided to narrow it down to Moscow, Russia. I chose a city at random. This data set came from Kaggle.com, uploaded by Berkeley Earth. Climate change is still being debated when it’s not a matter of if it’s happening, but the severity of it. I care a lot about how we are treating the earth and think we need to focus on trying to make this place better, rather than a plan b on a different planet.

A lot of rhetoric around climate change tends to focus on if humans are the cause of it. At this point, fault isn’t what matters, and we should consider if we have the responsiblity to fix it, since we can see it happening, as shown in the graph.
